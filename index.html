Grilled Cheese Sandwich Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 4 slices of cheese
    • cheddar
      • Tillamook
        • Sharp
    • mozzarella
    • pepper jack
  • 1 tbsp of butter

Grilled Cheese Sandwich Instructions:

  1. Spread butter on bread and frying pan
  2. Place bread in frying pan and fry
  3. Add cheese on top of bread
  4. Cover with second slice of bread
  5. Turn over and fry for 2 minutes
  6. (Optionally):
    1. Add a few drops of Tabasco sauce on top of sandwich
    2. Cut sandwich in one of three ways:
      1. Diagonally
      2. Horizontally
      3. Vertically
